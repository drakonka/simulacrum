Instructions

Your best friend [bffname] the snail has disappeared from your garden patch! You have searched far and wide, but it is nowhere to be found.

Sensing yourself falling into a deep depression over the loss of your friend, you decide to take matters into your own hands by breeding a snail who looks just like [bffname]!

You have until the end of summer, 15 days, to produce your best effort.

Proximity to your best friend is governed mostly by the genetic traits they have in common and only slightly by the specific color proximity of various traits. A 100% identical clone of your BFF will have a perfect proximity score of 26. Pay attention to the potential gene combinations to try to breed for your desired traits.
